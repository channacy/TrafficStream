Traffic Stream
Collecting Daily Traffic Data For Philadelphia and Baltimore

Traffic refers to the movement of vehicles and pedestrians on roads, streets, and highways. It is a vital aspect of transportation infrastructure and plays a significant role in the functioning of cities and communities. Understanding traffic patterns and trends is essential for urban planning, traffic management, and improving overall transportation efficiency.

Traffic data, including real-time and historical information, is collected through various methods such as traffic cameras, sensors, and GPS devices. Analyzing this data helps transportation authorities make informed decisions regarding traffic flow, road maintenance, and infrastructure development.

The purpose of this Traffic Dashboard is to provide users with access to both real-time and historical traffic data. By visualizing this information, users can gain insights into traffic conditions, identify patterns, and make informed decisions about their travel routes and timing.
